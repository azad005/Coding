Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about ExpressVPN.

What is a VPN and why do I need one?

A VPN (Virtual Private Network) encrypts your internet connection and routes it through a secure server, hiding your IP address and protecting your online activity from hackers, ISPs, and surveillance. You need a VPN to protect your privacy on public Wi-Fi, access geo-restricted content, and prevent tracking of your online activities.

How does ExpressVPN protect my privacy?

ExpressVPN uses AES-256 encryption (the same standard used by governments and banks), operates a strict no-logs policy (we never track or store your online activity), and offers features like a kill switch and DNS leak protection to ensure your privacy is maintained at all times.

Can I use ExpressVPN on multiple devices?

Yes! Depending on your plan, you can connect between 1 to unlimited devices simultaneously. We offer easy-to-use apps for Windows, Mac, iOS, Android, Linux, and routers. You can also manually configure ExpressVPN on many other devices.

Will ExpressVPN slow down my internet?

While all VPNs add some overhead due to encryption, ExpressVPN is optimized for speed. Our global network of high-speed servers ensures minimal impact on your connection speed. Many users report being able to stream HD video and play online games with no noticeable lag.

What's your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee with no questions asked. If you're not completely satisfied with ExpressVPN, simply contact our support team within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.
